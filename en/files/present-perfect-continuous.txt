📌 Have been doing 📌

Ketika kita bilang have been waiting, have been reading, have been watching, dll itu berarti kita menyatakan durasi. Udah berapa lama sih kita ngelakuin itu.


✅Saya sudah membaca buku ini selama 3 hari ➡️ I have been reading this book for 3 days.



✅Dia sudah menunggumu bertahun-tahun ➡️ She has been waiting for you for years.


✅Mereka sudah bermain badminton sejak Rudi datang ➡️ They have been playing badminton since Rudi came this morning.
