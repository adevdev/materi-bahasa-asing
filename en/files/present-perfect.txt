📌 Have Done 📌

Ketika kita bilang have done, have made, have told, dll itu berarti simply bilang saya sudah.


✅Saya sudah mandi ➡️ I have taken a bath


✅Saya sudah mengerjakan PR ➡️ I have done my homework


✅Dia sudah sarapan ➡️ She has had breakfast.



Tapi hati-hati❗️❗️❗️

Jangan menggunakan have done dengan yesterday, ...ago, last .... Karena itu kurang tepat. Kecuali ada since (sejak) atau for (selama)
