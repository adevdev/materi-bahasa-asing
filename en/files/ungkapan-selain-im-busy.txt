⭐ *Ungkapan* ⭐
Ungkapan-Ungkapan Alternatif "I am Busy"

💡 *I'm / I am busy* berarti "Saya sedang sibuk". Ungkapan-Ungkapan lainnya yang bermakna sama yaitu:

● I'm busy as a bee.
● I'm slammed (with/at work).
● I'm buried in work.
● I'm overwhelmed by/with work.
● I'm up to my ears in work.
● I've got a lot on my plate.
● I'm behind on my work.
● I'm tied up.
● I'm snowed under with work.
