⭐ *Ungkapan* ⭐
_(Something) along the lines (of)_

*Arti:* semacam/seperti/sejenis

*Serupa:*
• something like
• something similar to

📚 *Contoh Kalimat:*

● _They’re trying to organize a trip to the beach or *something along those lines.*_
= Mereka sedang merencanakan wisata ke pantai atau *semacam itu*.

● _We usually start with general questions *along the lines* of, ‘How do you feel?’_
= Kami biasanya mulai dengan pertanyaan umum *semacam* 'Bagaimana perasaanmu?'

● _In fact, I was thinking I might try and start *something along those lines* as soon as I can._
= Kenyataannya, aku habis berpikir aku mungkin akan mencoba dan memulai *hal sejenis itu* begitu aku bisa.
