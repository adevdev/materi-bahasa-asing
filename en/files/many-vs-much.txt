Many vs Much


Many dan much memiliki makna banyak. Yang membedakannya adalah countable dan uncountable nouns.


🍓Countable noun menggunakan many

Contoh: 
Aku punya banyak kucing

I have many cats.


Dia telah menulis banyak buku.

She has written many books.



🍓 Uncountable noun menggunakan much


Contoh:
Aku tidak menambahkan banyak garam.

I didn't add much salt.


Dia minum banyak air setiap hari.

He drinks much water every day.
