☆ Inversion ☆
◇ Pertukaran Subjek dan Verb ◇

Dalam bahasa Inggris subject dan auxiliary verb bisa bertukar. Ini terjadi pada beberapa bentuk kalimat, salah satunya kalimat tanya.

🚗 *Aturan Inversion*
Pertukaran Biasa (Subj-Aux)
• You will      --> Will you
• He can      --> Can he
• We should --> Should we
• The man has --> Has the man

Jika kalimatnya tidak memiliki auxiliary, maka ketika bertukar, gunakan auxiliary *do/does/did* tergantung jenis subjeknya dan tense. Perhatikan bahwa jika auxiliary-nya sudah ada maka kata kerja kembali ke bentuk dasar (misalnya "see").
Present Tense:
• *I* see        --> *Do I* see
• *You* see   --> *Do you* see
• *We* see    --> *Do we* see
• *They* see  --> *Do they* see
• *He* sees   --> *Does he* see
• *She* sees  --> *Does she* see
• *It* sees     --> *Does it* see
• *The person* sees --> *Does the person* see
• *The people* see --> *Do the people* see

Past Tense:
• *I* saw       -->  *Did I* see
• *You* saw  --> *Did you* see
• *He* saw   -->  *Did he* see
• *The person* saw --> *Did the person* see
• *The people* saw --> *Did the people* see
(Sama semua menggunakan did)

Jika menggunakan *to be* tanpa auxiliary maka subjek bertukar dengan to be itu.
• I am         -->    Am I
• You are   -->    Are you
• We are     -->   Are we
• They are  -->   Are they
• He is        -->   Is he
• She is      -->    Is she
• It is          -->    Is it
• The man is     -->  Is the man
• The men are  -->  Are the men

Jika ada lebih dari satu auxiliary maka yang pertama saja yang bertukar dengan subjek.
• She would have --> Would she have

🚕 *Kalimat-Kalimat Yang  Menggunakan Inversion*

1) Kalimat Tanya (kecuali yang menanyakan subjek)
• _You collect stamps._
》 _What *do you* collect?_
• _He will come at night._
》 _When *will he* come?_

Kecuali yang menanyakan subjek. Tidak ada inversion:
• _He is her father._
》 _Who is her father?_

2) Kalimat Dengan Keterangan Negatif/Pembatas di Awal
(Bahasa formal)
• _I have never seen such a beautiful scenery._
》 _Never *have I* seen such a beautiful scenery._

• _I understood why the tragedy had happened only then._
》 _Only then *did I* understand why the tragedy had happened._

3) Kalimat Pengandaian yang Menggunakan Had:
(Bahasa formal)
• _If we had gotten there in time, we could have prevented the accident from happening._
》 _*Had we* gotten there in time..._

4) Kalimat dengan Keterangan Tempat di Awal.
(Bahasa formal/sastra)

Inversion pada kalimat ini terjadi langsung pada subjek dengan semua verbnya yang ada.
• _Two farmers lived in the village._
》 _In the village *lived two farmers.*_

• _A magic tree is on the other side of the river._
》 _On the other side of the river *is a magic tree.*_

• _A bottle will be in the box._
》 _In the box *will be a bottle.*_

5) Kalimat dengan So+adjective...that di Bagian Awal
• _The girl was so beautiful that nobody could talk of anything else._
》 _So beautiful *was the girl* that nobody could ...._

6) Kalimat dengan kata So (Begitu Juga)
• _I do, too._
》 _So *do I.*_

• _That guy has, too._
》 _So *has that guy.*_
