SOME OTHER WAYS TO GREET PEOPLE
biasanya kita menyapa seseorang dengan menanyakan kabar. Dan di SD, kita sudah diperkenalkan dengan How are you?

Padahal banyak cara untuk menanyakan kabar biar anti-mainstream gitu deh..... 

Berikut cara menanyakan kabar:
1. How are you? 
2. How are you doing? 
3. What's up? / Whassup? / sup? (Bahasa chatting) / Whaddup?
4. What are you up to? (tapi ini bisa bermakna ganda antara gimana kabarnya sama apa yang akhir2 ini kamu lakukan)
5. How have you been? / How have you been doing? 
6. How's life? 
7. How's everything? 
8. What's new?
9. What's the good word? / what's the word? 

Contoh:
Paijo: Hi, Man! I haven't seen you in ages. What's the good word? 
Paiman : Nothing much

Paijo: Hai, Man! Udah lama gak ketemu. Gimana kabarnya nih?
Paiman: Sama kayak biasanya

Parto: Hi, Sule! How have you been doing? 
Sule : Peachy. What about you? 
Parto : I am pretty good. 

Parto: Hai, Sule! Pa kabar nih?
Sule: Baik. Kamu sendiri gimana?
Parto: Lumayan baik
