⭐ *Ungkapan* ⭐

🇬🇧 if it weren't for [someone/something]
🇮🇩 jika saja bukan karena [seseorang/sesuatu]

Contoh:
》 _*If it weren't for* you, I wouldn't be here today._
= *Jika bukan karena* kamu, aku tidak akan ada di sini sekarang.

Ungkapan serupa:
• If it hadn't been for ...
• Without ...
