*#GRAMMAR LESSON*

📌 Neither, Either, So, Too 📌
 

Keempat kata ini kalau diartikan secara harfiah ke dalam Bahasa Indonesia akan jadi..... 

aku juga

Contoh:
1. A: Aku suka dia
    B: Aku juga

2. A: Mia gak biasa tidur di ruangan ber-AC. 
    B: Dina juga


Gampang kan? 


Lalu, Bahasa Inggrisnya gimana? 
.
.
🦋 So dan Too digunakan untuk kalimat positif.

Rumusnya:

🐠 *So + auxiliary verb + Subject*

🐠 *Subject + auxiliary verb + too*

For examples :

❄Damon : You know what? I wanna kill you. (simple present)

Stefan : I do too / so do I
.
.
❄Dad: Let's go. I have had breakfast. (present perfect) 
Mom: Nina has too / so has Nina.

❄Tukul : I will go to Europe next week. (simple future) 
Sule : I will too/ So will I
.
.
.
🦋 Either dan Neither digunakan untuk kalimat negative. 
Rumusnya:

🐠 *Subject + auxiliary verb + not + either*

🐠 *Neither + auxiliary verb + Subject*

For examples :

❄Jono: I didn't attend the class yesterday. (simple past) 
Joni : I didn't either / neither did I
.
.
❄Dono : Shinta hasn't come yet. (Present perfect)
Doni : Sheila hasn't either / neither has Sheila
